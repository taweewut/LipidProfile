The Most Important Metric: TG/HDL Ratio for Thai Individuals

While standard lipid panels are useful, the Triglyceride/HDL (TG/HDL) ratio is a more powerful predictor of cardiovascular risk, especially for Asian populations. Research shows that Thai individuals may face increased risk at lower TG/HDL ratios than Western populations. This phenomenon, known as the "lean Asian paradox," means a more stringent interpretation is necessary.

A Holistic View of Your Health

Cardiovascular health doesn't exist in a vacuum. It is deeply interconnected with your metabolic health, kidney function, and inflammatory status. Monitoring these markers provides a complete picture of your body's overall wellness.
